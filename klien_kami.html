Started as Makassar Shipyard Projects in 1963, is 100% owned by the government of Indonesia, founded on 29 October 1977. Known as PT IKI and headquartered in Makassar, South Sulawesi.

PT IKI is the biggest shipyard in eastern Indonesia with yards in Makassar and Bitung, North Sulawesi.
